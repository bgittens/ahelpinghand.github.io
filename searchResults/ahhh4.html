John F Kennedy:
Profile in Courage
Essay Contest

Description

The John F. Kennedy Library Foundation invites U.S. high school students to describe and analyze an act of political courage by a U.S. elected official who served during or after 1917, the year John F. Kennedy was born. The Profile in Courage Essay Contest opens on September 1, 2019. The deadline is January 17, 2020.

Requirements

You must be a high school student to enter this competition.
